Musical Interests

Nontraditional students are adult, veteran and/or part-time learners, including returning adults whose formal education has been interrupted, students needing to purse higher education on a part-time basis, post-baccalaureate students pursuing a second undergraduate degree or major, and non-degree students or those students with significant non-academic commitments: being self-supporting, having full-time employment (34 hours/week or more), or having financial dependents. Specialized academic advising, evening classes, and co-curricular programming geared to the needs and interests of returning adult or part-time students is available. Nontraditional students are invited to affiliate with University College, which provides specialized programs and services to promote the academic success of nontraditional students are foster a strong sense of community.
