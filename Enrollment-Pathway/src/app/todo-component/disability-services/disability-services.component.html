Students With Disabilities

Students with disabilities interested in receiving accommodations should contact the Office of Disability Services at 848-445-6800, 1-800-852-7899(TTY) or ods@echo.rutgers.edu. If you would like to receive more information about services for students with disabilities, please indicate "Yes". Doing so will enable us to assist you in a timely manner. Information on the Office of Disability Services is available at http://ods.rutgers.edu.
