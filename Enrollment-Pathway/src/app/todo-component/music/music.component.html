Student Life

Getting involved in on-campus activities assists new students with their transition to college life. It helps in meeting new students and making new friends; identifying new interests; developing new skills; having access to more resources and services; and increasing satisfaction with the first year experience.

All of our choirs and the Marching Band are open to ALL Rutgers students, not just music majors. These are also openings in all other ensembles each year for non-majors.
