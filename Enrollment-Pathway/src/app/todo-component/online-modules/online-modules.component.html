New Student Online Modules

All incoming first-year and transfer students are required to complete these two online learning modules. Because we will be discussing information in the modules as part of New Student Orientation, we are asking all incoming first-year and transfer students to complete the two online learning modules before attending orientation. The modules must be completed by September 1. Any student who fails to complete the two modules by the September 1 will have a registration hold placed on their account and will be unable to register for spring classes until both modules are completed.

Not Anymore is an online program designed to help students foster healthy relationships and deal with the issues of sexual assault, sexual harassment, dating violence, and stalking. 

Not Anymore for Alcohol & Other Drugs is designed to inform students about the risks of drug and alcohol abuse. Students will learn a variety of methods to manage situations involving these substances.

To access the online learning modules:

  • Follow this link to the modules.
  • Select "NetID Login."
  • Enter your NetID and Password.
  • Click on "Required Programs."
  • Complete Not Anymore and Not Anymore for Alcohol & Other Drugs before you attend orientation.
